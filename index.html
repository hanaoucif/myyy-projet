About Our Center

We offer a play-based emergent curriculum complete with low teacher
to child ratios. Come see our environment and how it is designed to
promote exploration, adventure and imaginative play. We believe that
children are active participants in their own learning. That’s why we
encourage them to ask questions and explore the world around them.
